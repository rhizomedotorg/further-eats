Further
Experiments
in
Art & Technology

This event showcases new works created artists Andrew Demirjian, Foreign Objects, Kinlaw, Fei Liu, Sarah Rothberg, and Mark Ramos & Ziyang Wu as part of a unique collaboration between NEW INC, Rhizome, and the Experiments in Art & Technology program at Nokia Bell Labs. It also offers a platform to discuss the state of art-tech interaction and the opportunities of interdisciplinary work between engineers and artists envisioning deep futures and recovering essential pasts.
